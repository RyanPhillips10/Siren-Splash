What is SIREN?

In total, there are over 500,000 trucking accidents that injure 130,000 people every year. Recognizing that a quarter of these accidents are weather-related, SIREN created RoadWatch to inform large companies’ truck drivers and their managers about hazardous road conditions.

What does SIREN do for you?

SIREN will boost your company’s safety and efficiency by automating the way your company analyzes and distributes weather information. Its use will keep your assets safe, give your drivers accurate and timely information, and relieve your dispatchers of the distraction caused by focusing on weather data.

How does SIREN work?

With the help of several meteorologists, we have developed a program that takes in weather data and notifies you when road conditions are expected to be dangerous. These notifications are automatically sent out to drivers through your fleet’s existing communication infrastructure. Having access to SIREN’s system will free up your driver managers to work on other important tasks, and ensure that your drivers have the best information when they need to make decisions on the road.
