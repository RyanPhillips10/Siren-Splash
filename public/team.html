Management Team

Ryan Phillips

CEO & VP of Technology

Ryan is a Computer Science major with a minor in Mathematics at the University of Oklahoma. He brings in experience from 3 semesters of developing technologies at the Center for the Creation of Economic Wealth in fields ranging from book publishing to patient data monitoring. As an intern at Garmin, his work on the next-generation of in-dash navigation prepared Ryan to bring a large scale software project from ideation through production. Ryan led the ideation of SIREN idea, pulling together contacts from many disciplines to aid in the project. He will serve as the interim CEO until an established software CEO can be brought in at a later time. He will then serve as the VP of technology, drawing from his experience with internships at Garmin and Microsoft.

Drew Knox

VP of Sales & Marketing

Drew is an Entrepreneurship and Venture Management major with a minor in Marketing at the University of Oklahoma. He brings a great deal of experience from his time as Interfraternity Council President and from serving as the co-founder, President, and leading Sales Executive for The Odyssey at Oklahoma. Further, marketing experiences as an intern for MRY and for the Oklahoma City Thunder have prepared Drew to take on these responsibilities. His management, sales, and marketing expertise will allow Drew to effectively hire the firm’s sales force and to manage all sales and promotional activities for the company.

Nick Ortiz

VP of Business Development

Nick is an Entrepreneurship and Venture Management major at the University of Oklahoma. He has significant experience in startup business development from co-founding OU’s first Greek life newspaper, developing creative sales and marketing campaigns across 6 campuses for Trellise (a student organizational software startup), and his involvement in the original business ideation team for SIREN through the Center for the Creation of Economic Wealth at OU. His previous work with the business development of startups has prepared him to lead SIREN through launch.

Robert Byrd

VP of Public Relations

Robert is a Finance and Zoology major at the University of Oklahoma. A member of the SIREN founding team, he has continued with the project as head of Operations. He first gained significant client-facing experience as an Associate Consultant with a tax agency based in Dallas. Drawing from the skills gained in this role, Robert has been instrumental in the process of developing corporate partnerships and promoting the SIREN program to potential customers. Robert will continue to assist in the external development of the venture, and will oversee the continued improvement and distribution of SIREN’s software. His understanding of clients’ needs, together with his experience in ideating and implementing the solution to those needs, has equipped him to fill this role.

Andrew Tierney

VP of Finances

Andrew is a Finance and Accounting major at the University of Oklahoma. He is a part of the founding business ideation team for SIREN and has been involved in the continued commercialization ever since. Currently interning for the Office of Technology Development on OU’s research campus he is engaged in a software commercialization project as the entrepreneurial lead. Additional experience includes his current management of Microsoft and Coca Cola’s collegiate marketing campaigns. This summer he will intern for Ernst & Young’s Financial Services Office in NYC, which will give him the financial skills needed to successfully bring SIREN to market.
